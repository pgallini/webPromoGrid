Why Promo Grid?

You need to know which associates need development and which ones are ready for a Promotion. 

Works everywhere

You are always on the go – your performance management tools need to be as well. Promo Grid makes it easy to maintain great data points on your employees throughout the year. Performance management should be an on-going journey – not a rushed job done at the end of the year.

Avoid the Peter Principal

The Peter Principal warns us to avoid a situation where "managers rise to the level of their incompetence." In addition to a person’s performance, you need to consider their capacity to take on more responsibility.  Promo Grid makes it easy to rate individuals across two dimensions revealing those ready for promotion.
